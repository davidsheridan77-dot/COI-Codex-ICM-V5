## Revenue Strategy

Revenue generation aligns with the V5 build phases. COI does not chase revenue prematurely — the platform must be solid first.

**Sequence:**
1. P1-P5 — Build the core platform (chat, routing, voice, PC control, gaming mode)
2. P6-P7 — Extend reach (remote access, media)
3. P8-P9 — Package for distribution (setup wizard, auto-updates)
4. Revenue launches when the platform is stable and distributable
5. Revenue funds hardware upgrades
6. Hardware upgrades expand COI's capability
7. Expanded capability enables more sophisticated revenue streams

---

## Revenue Rules
1. Hardware fund is fed first from any revenue
2. No paid tools until revenue supports them
3. Every income stream must be automatable eventually
4. Track everything — no guessing on numbers
5. Revenue strategy is not improvised — it follows the build order
