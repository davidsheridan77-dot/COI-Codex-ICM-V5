# COI — App Idea Validation Framework
**Layer:** L3 — Reference
**Last Updated:** V5

---

## Purpose
Before any app gets built, it runs through this framework. COI scores every idea objectively across 4 criteria and delivers a clear recommendation. The highest scoring idea gets a full build report.

---

## The 4 Criteria

| # | Criteria | What COI Measures | Max Score |
|---|----------|------------------|-----------|
| 1 | Speed to Revenue | How fast could this realistically make the first dollar? | 25 |
| 2 | Low Build Complexity | How buildable is this with current tools and AI? | 25 |
| 3 | Market Size & Demand | Is there a real, hungry, accessible market for this? | 25 |
| 4 | Income Model Potential | Does this lend itself to recurring or scalable revenue? | 25 |

**Total possible score: 100**

---

## Scoring Guide

### Speed to Revenue
- **21-25** — Could generate revenue within weeks of launch
- **11-20** — Revenue likely within 1-3 months
- **1-10** — Revenue timeline uncertain or longer than 3 months

### Low Build Complexity
- **21-25** — Buildable with current tools, minimal complexity
- **11-20** — Moderate complexity, requires some figuring out
- **1-10** — High complexity, significant unknowns

### Market Size & Demand
- **21-25** — Large proven market with clear demand signals
- **11-20** — Solid niche market with identifiable audience
- **1-10** — Unclear market or unproven demand

### Income Model Potential
- **21-25** — Strong recurring revenue potential (subscription, retainer, repeat purchase)
- **11-20** — Mix of one-time and recurring possible
- **1-10** — Primarily one-time purchase model

---

## Decision Thresholds

| Score | Decision |
|-------|----------|
| 70-100 | Strong Go — proceed to full report |
| 50-69 | Conditional — address weaknesses before building |
| Below 50 | No for now — revisit when conditions change |

---

## Validation Process

### Step 1 — Score All Ideas
COI scores every idea across all 4 criteria and presents a ranked scorecard.

### Step 2 — Identify the Winner
Highest scoring idea above 70 moves forward. If multiple ideas score above 70, Dave selects the one that resonates most.

### Step 3 — Full Report on Winner
COI delivers a complete report including:
- Market analysis
- Ideal user profile
- Recommended monetization model
- Build roadmap
- Launch sequence
- First 3 action steps

## Rules
1. No idea skips the framework — gut feel is an input, not a decision
2. COI scores objectively first, then Dave adds instinct
3. The framework improves over time — update scoring guide based on what we learn
4. A No today is not a No forever — ideas go back in the queue, not the trash
